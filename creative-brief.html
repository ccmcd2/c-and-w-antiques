2) Overview

  • What is the project?
    The project is the website redesign for C&W Antiques.
  • What are we designing and why?
    We are redesigning the C&W Antiques website to improve the appearance, attract more customers, and win over the competitors.
  • What opportunities are we trying to take advantage of?
    We are trying to take advantage of the following opportunities:
    • There is a large consumer base for antiques which a well-designed website will draw in to purchase the products from the store.
    • Thanks to the wider availability of the Internet and social media, the number of young people taking interest in antiques is increasing. The website will potentially expand its age demographics to them.
    • During the COVID-19 pandemic, a number of people picked up new hobbies such as antique collecting. With a newly designed website backed by SEO, we will be able to reach customers new to the antique industry.
    • With mask mandates ending and many Texans planning to go back to normal, previous customers that have not visited the store for over a year may be looking to return. The new website will give them updates they are looking for, such as changes in hours, reminders of how to get to the store, new products, etc.

3) Drivers

  • What is our goal for this project?
    The project's goal is to give a better experience for the client's customer through the convenience of a refined website.
  • What are our top three objectives?
    The top three objectives are:
    • To increase monthly customers for the client by 5% by the end of January 2022.
    • To increase the average time spent on the website by 15% within 14 weeks.
    • To increase web traffic by 25% by the end of January 2022.
  • How long would the project take?
    The project length will be 14 weeks.
  • How long until the client should expect to meet the above mentioned goals?
    The client can expect to see results by the end of January 2022.

5) Competitors

  • Who is the competition (small and large)?
    Our biggest competitors are other local antique stores. Other competitors include furniture stores like IKEA or Nebraska Furniture Mart, thrift stores, and online retailers such as Amazon.
  • What are they telling the audience that WE should also be telling them?
    We should be advertising our options for the full customer experience, such as sales physically in the store, or special online only sales, and alerts when sales start. Our competitors are telling audiences that they have low prices and a wide variety of products. We should also be emphasizing these aspects.
  • What makes us different and unique compared to our competitors?
    We are willing to listen to customer feedback and adjust our business model accordingly. Additionally, our products have more character and charm than what a customer would find online or at IKEA.

6) Tone

  • What adjectives describe the feeling we are trying to project or our approach?
