About us

We have been in the antique and fine arts business for well over 15 years. Our brand has evolved as we transitioned from renovating upscale properties to operating an upscale retail antique store in the Dallas/Fort Worth metroplex.

Meet the Owners

Jim Carpenter

Jim Carpenter, co-proprietor of C&W Antiques graduated with a degree in business administration and grew up in the homebuilding industry. When Jim moved to DFW, he and his business partner began renovating large properties and staging them with English and French fine antique furnishings and art.

Patrick Walsh

Patrick Walsh, co-proprietor of C&W Antiques, was born in Paris France and has lived throughout Europe. This is where he developed his passion for European antiques and his eye for the English and French manner style. After graduating from the University of Richmond, Patrick moved with his job to the Dallas/Fort Worth metroplex.
